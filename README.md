This repository is provided as an open scientific resource to facilitate understanding, verification, and reproduction of the theoretical derivations and numerical simulations presented in the associated publications. The materials are intended solely for academic and research purposes.

Although no copyright restrictions are imposed on the use of these materials, users are kindly requested to cite the corresponding manuscript(s) when employing any part of the code, derivations, or simulation results in academic publications, presentations, or derivative works. Proper citation ensures appropriate attribution and supports reproducible research practices.

1. CMQT.ipynb contains analytical derivations and numerical simulation routines corresponding to the manuscript
“Coherence-mediated quantum thermometry in a hybrid circuit-QED architecture”,
available at: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2510.16318

2. TBQC.ipynb contains analytical derivations and numerical simulation routines corresponding to the manuscript
“Two-tooth bosonic quantum comb for temporal-correlation sensing”,
available at: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2601.10916
